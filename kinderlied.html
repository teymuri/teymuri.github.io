Kinderlied (2014)

Language Composition

Dur.: 25'

Voice: Marlene Behrmann

Amir Teymuri · Kinderlied – Musik für Lautsprecher

"Kinderlied" is an acousmatic piece which evolves as a continuous process exclusively from the whispered text exposed at the beginning. The text used is the german children's song "Hänschen klein".
