Bytebeat (2023)

Für Computerflügel

Dauer: 4'09''

For this piece I executed a short Python script multiple times to gain randomly generated stream of data for different ranges of the instrument. These data embody both diverse binary rhythms (only negative indices of 2, e.g. 2^0 for a quarter note, 2^-1 for an eighth note, 2^-2 for a sixteenth note etc.) and the pitches, which consist of the three notes of a C major chord (C, E, G) spread over the entire ambitus of the keyboard (from C1 upto C8).
