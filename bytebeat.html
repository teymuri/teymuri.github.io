For this piece I executed a short Python script several times with varying parameters, to gain randomly generated data which embodied both the rhythm (only binary durations with negative indices of 2, e.g. 2^0 for a quarter note, 2^-1 for an eighth note, 2^-2 for a sixteenth note etc.) and pitches which consist only of the three notes of a C major chord (C, E, G) spread over the entire ambitus of the keyboard (from C1 up to C8). These data layers were then arranged manually through juxtaposition and superimposition to form different sections of the composition.
