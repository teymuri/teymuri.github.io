Cresce La Pena Mia (2023)

für Saxophonquartett

Dauer: 54"

Cresce la Pena Mia is a reimagining of Bartolomeo Tromboncino's Renaissance-era frotolla. The aim was to craft a fresh sonic experience while preserving the original frotolla's harmonic and rhythmic structures.
